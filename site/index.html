It's completely free!

Quizfreely is effective without being expensive. It has no ads, pop-ups, free trails, limits, or paid features. It's free, open source, and doesn't even require an account!

It's open source!

Quizfreely is open source. That means it's publicly developed, so everyone can view and contribute to its source code. Quizfreely isn't owned by any company either, so it doesn't make money off of its users.
